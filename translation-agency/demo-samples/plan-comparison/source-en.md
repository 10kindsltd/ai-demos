# Choosing your Warden plan

Warden Home and Warden Business both include real-time protection and a firewall, so
every device stays covered from the moment it connects.

Warden Home protects up to 5 devices for $4.99 per month. Warden Business scales to 50
devices and adds centralized management for your whole team.

Every plan includes ransomware protection and automatic updates, and you can manage all
your devices from the dashboard.

You can cancel your subscription any time — no lock-in, no cancellation fees.
